Dr. Joseph M. Bennett, DC

Doctor of Chiropractic • Palmer College of Chiropractic, Class of 1975

For over four decades, Dr. Joseph M. Bennett has been Fayette, Alabama's trusted chiropractor. After graduating from the prestigious Palmer College of Chiropractic in Davenport, Iowa—the founding institution of chiropractic care—Dr. Bennett brought his expertise home to serve his community.

Dr. Bennett's approach focuses on treating the source of pain, not just masking symptoms. Using time-tested chiropractic techniques combined with modern understanding of musculoskeletal health, he helps patients achieve lasting relief and improved quality of life.

Beyond his practice, Dr. Bennett has been a pillar of the Fayette community. His decades of service with the Boy Scouts of America has helped shape young leaders throughout Fayette County. He remains actively involved in local philanthropic initiatives, embodying the values of service, integrity, and community care that define both his practice and his character.

Our Services

Comprehensive Chiropractic Care

Expert diagnosis and treatment for a wide range of musculoskeletal conditions. We focus on restoring proper spinal alignment to relieve pain and improve your overall health.

Back Pain Treatment

Targeted spinal adjustments to relieve chronic and acute lower back pain. We identify misalignments causing nerve compression and restore proper vertebral position.

Conditions treated: Herniated discs, muscle strains, degenerative disc disease, lumbar stenosis, sacroiliac joint dysfunction

Neck Pain & Stiffness

Gentle cervical adjustments to restore mobility and reduce pain. Modern lifestyles often create chronic neck tension that responds excellently to chiropractic care.

Conditions treated: Tech neck, whiplash, cervical radiculopathy, torticollis, cervical disc problems

Sciatica Relief

Specialized adjustments to relieve sciatic nerve compression. We address the root cause—typically lumbar misalignment or disc issues—to stop radiating leg pain.

Conditions treated: Piriformis syndrome, lumbar radiculopathy, disc herniation, spinal stenosis affecting sciatic nerve

Headache & Migraine Care

Many chronic headaches originate from cervical misalignment and muscle tension. Chiropractic adjustments can significantly reduce headache frequency and intensity.

Conditions treated: Tension headaches, cervicogenic headaches, migraines, cluster headaches, occipital neuralgia

Auto Accident Injuries

Specialized care for whiplash and collision-related injuries. Early treatment after an accident prevents chronic pain and accelerates recovery.

Conditions treated: Whiplash, soft tissue injuries, spinal trauma, post-accident muscle spasms, delayed onset pain

Sports Injury Rehabilitation

Get back in the game faster. We treat athletic injuries and help optimize performance through proper spinal alignment and joint mobility.

Conditions treated: Sprains, strains, repetitive motion injuries, joint dysfunction, sports-related back/neck pain

Shoulder & Joint Pain

Extremity adjustments and soft tissue techniques to restore range of motion and reduce inflammation in shoulders, elbows, wrists, hips, and knees.

Conditions treated: Frozen shoulder, rotator cuff issues, bursitis, tennis elbow, carpal tunnel symptoms

Wellness & Maintenance Care

Preventive care to maintain spinal health and overall wellness. Regular adjustments help prevent future problems and optimize your body's function.

Benefits: Improved posture, better sleep, enhanced mobility, stress relief, injury prevention

Work Injury Care

Comprehensive treatment for workplace injuries. We provide documentation for workers' compensation claims and focus on getting you back to work safely.

Conditions treated: Lifting injuries, repetitive strain, ergonomic-related pain, slip and fall injuries
